Our Story

Rooted in the perfume capital of India.

Founded by the Ayush family, Ayush Aromatic grew out of Kannauj — a town that has distilled attars and essential oils by the traditional deg-bhapka method for centuries. We carry that craft forward, pairing time-tested hydro-distillation with modern quality control.

Every batch is sourced from trusted farms and traders, distilled in-house, and tested before it leaves our facility — so the oil that reaches your lab or workshop is exactly what the label says it is.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

What is the minimum order quantity for bulk essential oils?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product — most essential oils and attars start from 1kg for trial orders, with no upper limit for bulk export orders. Contact us with your requirement for an exact quote.

Do you provide a certificate of analysis (COA) with each batch?

Yes. Every batch of oil we ship is GC-MS tested, and a certificate of analysis covering density, refractive index and composition is provided free with your order.

Can Ayush Aromatic manufacture a custom fragrance blend?

Yes, we formulate custom aroma compounds and attars to a client's brief for soap, incense, cosmetics and home-fragrance applications, including private-label packaging.

Which countries does Ayush Aromatic export to?

We currently export to 15+ countries including the UAE, USA and France, and handle all documentation and customs clearance in-house for a smooth delivery.
